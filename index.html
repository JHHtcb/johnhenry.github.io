About Me

Hi! I'm John Henry Heeks.

While my wife kicks back with another episode of Law & Order, I sneak off to this corner of the internet to build, tinker, and occasionally yell at CSS like it’s a missed 27-yard field goal in the 4th quarter.

This site is my digital sandbox - part tech training, part personal expression, and part escape from watching the Vikings invent bold new ways to lose. Think of it as a living project: always changing, never quite perfect, much like my golf swing.

Browse around, take in the scenery, and if something catches your eye - or if you just want to talk football, web design, or why putting is a cruel joke—drop me a line.
